A Solid Organization

The Society of Actuaries (SOA) is the largest global professional actuarial organization. With unmatched research and education, and over 26,000 of the highest skilled actuarial professionals, we prepare the individuals trusted to drive better business decisions and promote financial security in an ever-changing world. Educating actuaries. Impacting society.

Students, employers, and professionals

In 2015, the actuarial profession was the top-ranked job in the United States.* A career in demand, SOA actuaries enjoy even greater resources and opportunities.
When you are a member of the SOA you are backed by an organization recognized the world over for the strength of our global credentials, the rigor of our pathways and the reach of our network. Together, we work to advance the interests of actuarial professionals around the globe.
